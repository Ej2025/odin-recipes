Fried Rice Recipe

home

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a work or frying pan
and is usually mixed with other ingredients
such as eggs vegetables, seafood or meat.it is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to other dish.

    Complete ingredients for Fried Rice:

  • 4 cups of rice
  • 5 cups of sliced cabbage
  • 2 cups of sliced carrot
  • 2 cups of sliced green beans
  • Half cups of peas
  • 1 cup of sliced green pepper
  • Cow liver(300g)
  • 2kg of chicken
  • 200ml of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup of sliced onions

Preparation

Most fried rice start with you parboiling the rice and cooking the meat to be used.
