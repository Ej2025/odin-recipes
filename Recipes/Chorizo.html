Chorizo Pasta

This chorizo pasta recipe is quick easy and has only a handful of ingredient! it's ready in about 30 minutes.

Why You"ll Love It

This is a really Simple Pasta dish that's great when you just want some sausage pasta and a simple tomato sauce.it's not fancy, and the chorizo really shines through.It's one of those brilliant midweek meals that doesn't take alot of effort but taste delicious

What You Will Need:

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Chorizo
  • Onions and garlic
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Cream
