Eggs benedict Pizza Recipe

home

Eggs Benedict Pizza is a delicious twist on the classic brunch dish, combining a crispy pizza crust, creamy hollandaise sauce, poached eggs, and savory toppings.Here is how to make it.

ingredients

    For the Pizza Base

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Half cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Half cup shredded cheddar cheese

    • For the hollandaise sauce

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Half cup of unsalted butter(melted)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the Toppings

  • 4 eggs (poached)
  • 4 sliced Canadian bacon or ham
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives or parsley
  • Half tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Preparations

    Step 1: Prepare the Pizza Base

  1. Preheat your oven to 475`F
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness
  3. Transfer to a pizza stone or baking sheet and brush lightly with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and cheddar cheese evenly over the dough.
  5. Bake for 10-12minutes or until the crust is golden and crispy.

    6. Step 2: Make the hollandaise Sauce

  6. In a heatproof bowl,whisk together egg yolks and lemon juice until smooth.
  7. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water.
  8. Slowly whisk in the melted butter while continuing to mix.
  9. Add Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper, whisking until the sauce thickens.Remove from heat.

    10. Step 3: Poach the Eggs

  10. Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer and add a splash of vinegar.
  11. Crack each egg into a small bowl and gently slide it into the water.
  12. Poach for 3-4 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks remain runny.
  13. Use a slotted spoon to remove the eggs and place them on a paper towel to drain.

    14. Step 4: Assemble the Pizza

  14. Arrange the canadian bacon or ham evenly over the baked pizza crust.
  15. Carefully place the poached eggs on top.
  16. Drizzle with the hollandaise sauce.
  17. Garnish with chopped chives and red pepper flakes.

    18. Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

    Slice and serve immediately for a rich and indulgent breakfast pizza!
